# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

This project adopts the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org),
version 2.1, as its Code of Conduct. The full text is available at:

https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/

In short: we are committed to providing a welcoming, respectful, and
harassment-free experience for everyone who participates in this project,
regardless of background or identity. We expect all participants to act
professionally and considerately in all project spaces.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainer at
**sven.herrmann@thatscalaguy.de**. All reports will be reviewed and investigated
promptly and fairly, and the reporter's privacy will be respected.

Maintainers are responsible for clarifying standards and may take appropriate
corrective action in response to any behavior they deem inappropriate.
